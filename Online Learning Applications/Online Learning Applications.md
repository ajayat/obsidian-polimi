## Stochastic Multi-Armed Bandit

> [!important] Pseudo-regret
> $$ \mathcal{R}_t = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_{t\in[T]} \hat{l}(a_t) \right] - T \underset{a}{\min} \hat{l}(a) $$
>  - $\hat{l}(a)$ the expected loss of an arm $a$.
